Hobbies

As of now, quarantine has taken quite a toll on my productivity and I find many things boring now as I have limited options. Here I will be displaying some recent hobbies I’ve made in quarantine.


Reading

A hobby I developed in quarantine was reading. With so much free time and not being able to see friends, it’s accumulated a lot of stress and boredom and one of the cures I’ve found for myself was reading. Books I’ve found interest in are the Fallout by Todd Strasser and a book called Divergent by Veronica Roth. I usually find apocalyptic and dystopian genres in books because they make me really think about real-life conflicts and how we would solve these problems and if we would have a good outcome like the ones in the stories.






Music

Along with reading, listening to music recently in quarantine has helped me pass time with a more uplifted mood and sometimes helps me accomplish tasks without getting very frustrated. Some of my favourite artists are fiji blue, Daniel Caesar, and Clairo.
